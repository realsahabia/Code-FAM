ABOUT Code:FAM

AN INFORMAL COMMUNITY FOR DEVELOPERS OF ALL EXPERIENCE LEVELS
Members

Code:FAM is a community of individuals with diverse backgrounds, including non-traditional Coders, Data Scientists, and career-switchers. Our primary objective is to create the most exceptional community for Developers and other hobbies that involve coding. Our events typically attract a wide range of participants, including self-taught hackers, university students, startup developers, fintech professionals, bootcamp graduates, and non-traditional tech enthusiasts of all ages.

Expect to meet new people in our events, job referrals, people hacking on cool side projects, and to oblige by the Code of Conduct.

We're the #1 community in tech/software/web categories from Ghana and actively persevere to create a big impact. We build trust and friendships among developers and drives our motivation from the force multipliers of the 21st century.

Our product is a community for Coders that convenes in-person twice a month and maintains digital communication through Discord.

Our Values

Connections

Our platform organizes events that can be beneficial in achieving your goals. You can meet new people who may be able to assist you with any coding obstacles you may encounter, whether you are a software developer, someone who is learning to code, or simply enjoy programming.

Safety

At the top of our page, we have a Code of Conduct that is accessible to the public, and we strictly adhere to it. Our organizers and volunteers take necessary precautions to maintain a secure environment that does not allow outsiders, solicitors, or third-party recruiters.

Trust

Our goal is to host an event biweekly, and we rely on both qualitative and quantitative data to steer our processes and continuously enhance each event. Our aim is to maintain the frequency of events without any compromise on their quality, and we strive to improve with each iteration.

How We Work

1) Bring a laptop

You only need to bring a laptop with you. We are a welcoming community and we are excited to connect with developers who are interested in meeting other developers, regardless of their level of experience.

We offer comfortable seating, wifi, and the opportunity to connect with interesting people. In the future, we plan to organize job fairs, happy hours, and hackathons.

2) Group circle

At 2:30 pm, we gather in a circle to introduce ourselves and share the programming languages/frameworks we can assist with, as well as our areas of need.

Following this, there is an optional circle for job seekers and employers to connect with each other.

3) Coding session

We code together, support each other with side projects, exchanging ideas, and explore new libraries and frameworks. In addition, we take breaks to enjoy coffee and engage with fellow coders.

North Code collaborates with other developer groups that empower our community. Our team actively consider the insights gained from successful developer events.
